18.3 Probability models A probability model describes a random phenomenon by telling us which of the following?

  1. (a) Whether we are using data based or personal probabilities.

  2. (b) What outcomes are possible and how to assign probabilities to these outcomes.

  3. (c) Whether the probabilities of all outcomes sum to 1 or sum to a number different than 1.

  4. (d) All of the above.
