3.32 Belief in God. A Gallup Poll conducted in May 2011 reports that 92% of a sample of 509 adults said Yes when asked “Do you believe in God?”

  1. (a) Use the quick method to estimate the margin of error for an SRS of this size.

  2. (b) Assuming that this was a random sample, make a confidence statement about the percentage of all adults who believe in God.
