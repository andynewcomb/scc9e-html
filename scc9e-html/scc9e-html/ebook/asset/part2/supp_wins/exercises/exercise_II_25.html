7.75 7.42 6.75 7.42 8.50 8.67 10.17
8.75 8.58 8.67 9.17 9.08 8.83 8.67

  1. (a) Make a histogram of these drive times. Is the distribution roughly symmetric, clearly skewed, or neither? Are there any clear outliers? (Hint: See pages 249 and 251.)
