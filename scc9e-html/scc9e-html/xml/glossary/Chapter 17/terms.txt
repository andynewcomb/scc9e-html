random
Phenomenon when individual outcomes are uncertain but there is, nonetheless, a regular distribution of outcomes in a large number of repetitions.
probability
Describes the long&#150;term regularity of random phenomena. It is a number between 0 and 1 that describes the proportion of times an outcome would occur in a very long series of repetitions.
personal probability
A number between 0 and 1 that expresses an individual's judgment of how likely an outcome is.
